Welcome to Holbæk's new cinema complex with 3 modern halls.

The cinema opened on 12.12.12 on Parallelvej 35 with a view of Holbæk Fjord.

The cinema has a total of 60 seats with good comfort, optimal picture and a superb sound experience.

There is free parking in front of the cinema.

Before the film, there is the opportunity to have fun with family and friends in our café
and in the summer there is the opportunity to sit outside on our terrace, overlooking the fjord.

We look forward to giving you a great movie experience - Have fun!
